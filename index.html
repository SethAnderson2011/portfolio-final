About Me

My journey toward becomming an IT professional started at 21 years old when I joined the Navy and became an IT in the military for 6 years. After a year of IT school in Pensacola, Florida I moved to Hawaii for my first duty station.
I got to enjoy three years working there before moving to Washington state for two years still working as an IT for the Navy. After my contract ended I moved to Salt Lake City, Utah for about a year and a half working as and IT for a subcontractor of a company called DISA before making my way back to Michigan where I am originally from.
After moving back home I worked as a training and safety coordinator for a pallet production company and now I am a student currently enrolled at Bay College furthering my IT education. Learning to create and design web pages has been a great experience so far and now I get to share the project's I've created so far with you and I hope you enjoy them as much as I've enjoyed making them.
